Our Design Process

Take a look at our design process that helps us deliver customized and goal-oriented digital solutions.

Web Strategy and Planning

Before we put pen to paper (or start on your digital design, rather), we conduct thorough research into your industry, competitors and target market to gather insights that will inform your custom digital strategy.

In this phase, we:

Identify the ideal positioning for your brand

Collect target audience data and create user personas

Define valuable content and marketing channels

Define KPIs to measure performance

Creative Design

Heres where your website begins to come to life, as our award-winning designers step in. Your design is filled with color and custom elements as we incorporate your brands personality and tailor the user journey to your target market.

In this phase, we:

Design your custom website

Incorporate your unique branding elements

Present you with full-scale designs in Figma

Responsive Development

In the development phase, our expert development team works behind the scenes to build a custom, secure and scalable solution for your brand, on your chosen platform. From B2B to eCommerce, we ensure clean code and a website thats fully optimized.

In this phase, we:

Write and finalize coding for your website

Bring your custom solution to life in a secure staging site

Ensure a responsive solution for both desktop and mobile

Quality Assurance(QA)

Before we push your custom solution live for the world to see, we follow a stringent quality assurance process, leaving no stone unturned. We continuously test your sites speed, functionality and other key elements to make sure its ready for launch.

In this phase, we:

Perform meticulously testing across the frontend and backend

Ensure all features and functionalities are working as intended

Make changes as needed to ensure a superior user experience

Launch & Optimization

When our QA is complete, its time for launch! Our team will set up your server, carry out the DNS configuration and make sure all updates are finalized. Well provide Cloudflare security protection, along with both internal and external monitoring.

In this phase, we:

Perform final QA testing

Devise a marketing plan for launch

Push your new digital solution live
